Rules & Policies

  1. Respect each other, releasing PII(Personally Identifiable Information) regardless of with/without consent is strictly prohibited.(Including releasing your own!)
  2. No trolling, we understand having silly conversations, but doing so to the point of breaking other people's game or roleplay is punishable.
  3. Cheating, exploiting, and or hacking in the game servers can and will result in a permanent ban.
  4. Racism or any derogatory content including but not limited to, text, pictures, and videos.
  5. Any form of advertisement, without consent is strictly prohibited and will lead directly to disciplinary action (Poaching).
  6. Any form of content creation (streaming, videos, and pictures), we expect that you follow both of the policies and rules from the platform and our community.
  7. Discord is a requirement. If you leave the Discord server, it is considered a resignation. (Only applies to groups you have been added to.)
  8. Toxicity within the server, failing to comply with basic RP will result in an immediate punishment as we see fit.
  9. Powergaming, metagaming, and FailRP will result in punishment as we see fit.
  10. Spamming chat will result in an immediate punishment as we see fit.
  11. Spamming vehicles, props, and or anything considered spammable will result in an immediate punishment as we see fit.
